About

I've spent my life in the creative world: working in the music industry for almost two decades, playing in bands, spending my younger years working at an art museum, pursuing photography as a hobby, studying design fundamentals and learning the tools to create, both digital and analog artwork. More recently I've discovered a love of channeling my creative instincts, along with my ability to think logically and knack for problem solving, into software engineering. I've created this page to display the results of this discovery process.

I grew up and still live in Omaha, Nebraska with my wife and two young sons. I have a History degree from the University of Nebraska at Omaha and received a certification in Project Management from the University of Chicago. For over 17 years I worked at Saddle Creek, a world-renowned independent record label, as a project manager, director of various departments, coordinator of email marketing, and anything and everything else that landed on my plate. In my (very little) spare time, I enjoy music (listening and making), travel, film, photography, reading, visual art (appreciating and creating), being outdoors, and mostly just spending time with my family.
